It Keeps Tabs on Your Cat's Health by Changing Color

Cats are notorious for hiding illnesses, so it's often hard to tell if they are sick or in pain. PrettyLitter's health monitoring cat litter makes it easier for you to know if your feline is experiencing a potential health issue before urgent medical care is needed, saving you money, stress, and potentially your cat's life. Your cat’s health issues that can be associated with color change include:

  • Alkaline

    Certain types of urinary tract infections. High urinary pH can lead to bladder crystal and stone formation.

  • Acidity

    Metabolic acidosis & kidney tubular acidosis. Low urinary pH can lead to Calcium Oxalate.

  • Blood

    Bladder inflammation, bladder stones, urinary tract infection.
